Credit Card Transaction Fraud Detection
As I work in the Fintech domain, credit card transaction fraud detection
is a very critical issue

Although we have different means and measurements to flag a transaction as fraud
but I have seen in many cases we target legitimate customer or transaction

I have also come across scenarios where people/business are incorrectly declined at checkout
leading to frustration and lost business

The bottom line: 
better fraud detection means fewer losses for banks, 
less hassle for customers, and a harder time for criminals
